About the Project

A Living Estate in
the Western Ghats

Rite Canephora is a first-of-its-kind Luxury Villas & 5 Start Resort community in Sakaleshpur, where scenic coffee estates meet premium hospitality infrastructure.

Spread across 16 lush acres, it offers more than land ownership — it is an opportunity to own a part of the Western Ghats with access to 5-star resort amenities, plantation living, and a high-potential investment asset in one of Karnataka’s most sought-after getaway destinations.

Developed by Riteway & Medha Projects, trusted Bengaluru-based developers with a proven legacy in residential communities, Rite Canephora is partnered with a 5-star hospitality brand to ensure world-class resort management and guest experiences.

Rite Canephora is backed by a strategic 30-year partnership with one of the world’s largest resort operators, ensuring premium hospitality standards and elevated guest experiences within the project.

Sakaleshpur

A Destination
Unto Itself

Nestled in the Western Ghats, Sakaleshpur accounts for a third of India's cardamom production and is traditionally famous for trekking, coffee estates, and its 11th century Hoysala temple. Cool, misty, and perennially green.
